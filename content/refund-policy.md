# Refund Policy

## Refund Policy for Glow Up Planners

We are committed to providing high-quality digital planners to help you organize your life with GlowUp. Before you make a purchase, please take a moment to read our refund policy.

### Digital Product Refund Policy

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales made on GlowUp Planners are final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges on any digital purchases, as our products are instantly accessible and downloadable upon payment. Once a digital product has been purchased, it cannot be returned, and no refund will be provided.

### Why We Have This Policy

1. **Instant Access**: Once a purchase is completed, you gain immediate access to the digital files. These files cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.
2. **Intellectual Property**: Digital products are considered intellectual property. Once downloaded, the product cannot be retracted, ensuring the protection of our content.

### Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Although we do not offer refunds, if you encounter any issues with your digital purchase, please do not hesitate to contact our customer support team at [info@glowupplanners.com](mailto:info@glowupplanners.com).
